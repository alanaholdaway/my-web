My Story

Why I Love The Game

Basketball is more than a sport to me. I grew up watching Stephen Curry rewrite what was possible, and it changed how I think about effort, precision, and preparation.

From youth leagues to watching every Warriors playoff run since 2015, this game has shaped my competitive drive, my love of analytics, and my belief that system beats star power almost every time.

This page is my love letter to basketball!

The Dynasty

Why the Warriors Changed Everything

The 2015–2019 Warriors rewrote the rulebook. Here's what made them special:

  1. The Three-Point Revolution
    • Curry's range forced defenses to guard the entire half-court
    • Thompson complemented him as the most lethal catch-and-shoot scorer in history
    • The splash brothers redefined what "good shot selection" meant for an entire generation
  2. Death Lineup (Small Ball)
    • Green at center unlocked a switchable, pace-and-space lineup
    • Proved that positionless basketball could dominate traditional size
    • NBA-wide copycat effect: every team now chases versatile big men
  3. Collective Intelligence
    • Ball movement averaged 300+ passes per game, more than any other teaml
    • Steve Kerr's motion offense demanded trust and basketball IQ from every player
    • Draymond Green as the conductor: IQ over isolation
  4. Culture as Competitive Advantage
    • "Pound the rock" This Stoic philosophy grounded the team
    • Ego-free superstar coexistence: Curry, Thompson, Durant, Green, Iguodala
    • Player empowerment before it was a buzzword
  5. The Analytics Proof Point
    • First franchise to fully embrace the "no mid-range" offensive philosophy at scale
    • Efficiency metrics justified their style before results made it obvious
    • Inspired a league-wide shift toward data-informed roster construction
